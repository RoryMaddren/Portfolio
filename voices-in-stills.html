Installation
2025

Roles:
Graphic Designer, Photographer, UX/UI Designer, Sound Enginer, Composer, Joiner and Construction

Research and Prep.

Inspired by the overlaying technique of ---- and the interactive art of ---- we were interested in created a multifaceted experience driven design. Utilising sound, photography and motion tracking.

Research question - How might we... We began by researching Japanese photographer and planning the original photography trials for the mock line up.

Photography and Editing

The photography shoots were next. We shot 6 separate shoots, varying in style, colour, grain, background, pose and distance of the shot. This was in order to assist in the next stages as much as possible, allowing the strongest shot composition possible.

This was then refined in editing, using a basic multiplier adjustment to each photo to get a basic concept on how each photograph will sit on one another.

Through several test we found the eyes of each model became a distraction and gave an uneasy feeling. This was similar to the researched photographers work I found, however, through several experiments I found the elementating this aspect pf the final composition created a stronger and more appealing outcome.

Assembling Frame

We then moved into the assembly of the photography piece. By sliding the clear film photographs in between A4 sections of glass, we trialed several arrangements to test the composition of the full piece. Eventually after comparing 20 experimental arrangements we found the most visually captivating.

We found that overlaying a variety of distances and poses and backgrounds in this specific arrangement was strongest. Also by separating each photo with a piece of glass strengthened it further by creating an unexpected 3 dimensional effect when standing at different angles.

We built upon this by pressing the final to images in the same piece of glass. This added more variety and melded these two photos together more so, keeping it more abstract in the deepest portions of the image
