Interactive/UX & UI/Graphic Design

Kia Ora, I’m Rory a 25 year old interactive designer focused on creating eye capturing visuals and user focused design in the interactive space. Currently in my final year at Media Design School, I have dealt in a variety of styles and have begun to carve out my place in the design space. As a part-time photographer/videographer and musician I have a keen understanding and interest in both visuals and sound and tend to incorporate these aspects in my designs as when beneficial.

Outside of designing I am an avid supporter of the underground music community of Auckland both as a fan and as a photographer, and musician. So you can often find me wondering about at a local gig.
