Write Your Entry

We recommend if stuck on recounting your story to follow this structure. For the prototype version feel free to type anything.

1.

CONTEXT — Who is the person that you are separated from, and what do they mean to you and how did you separate?

2.

FEELING — How did you feel about the separation when it happened? What emotions were there at the time and did it feel justified?

3.

EFFECT — How do you feel about it now? Has there been strain, or if you are united again is it the same as it was?

4.

LEAVING THOUGHTS — How do you feel reflecting on it now? Has this strengthened your relationship? Where are you in the relationship presently?
